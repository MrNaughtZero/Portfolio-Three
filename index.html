About Me

Hello! My name is Neil McNaught. I'm a Web Developer. I'm from Liverpool, United Kingdom and I love everything tech. Since I was a young child I've been interested in Technology. My favourite tech at the moment has to be VR.

I first started out building very simple WordPress website. Now, I like to code from scratch. My favourite language at the moment is Python - especially using the Flask Framework.

When I'm not at my Mac coding, I like to spend my spare time being active. I enjoy visiting the gym (When Coronavirus isn't restricting me!!), spending times with friends and travelling the world. I have recently visited Amsterdam and Turkey.
